About SGGP

🎭 What is SGGP?

The Save Goth Girl Program is a community-driven initiative dedicated to preserving and celebrating goth culture, aesthetics, and identity. We discuss ideas to keep goth culture thriving, relevant, and accessible to new generations.

💜 Our Mission

To document goth culture, share knowledge, support community members, and explore how goth aesthetics and values continue to evolve while staying true to the subculture's roots.

🌙 Core Values

Inclusivity, creativity, self-expression, individuality, and community. We celebrate all forms of goth: romantic, gothic lolita, cybergoth, Victorian, and everything in between.

Meet the Founder

🖤 Sanjeev - Chief Goth Girl Rescuer

Mission Statement: "I saw goth girls being endangered by mainstream TikTok trends and I said NO. Not on my watch."

Why This Matters: It was a dark Tuesday morning when I realized goth culture needed a hero. Armed with nothing but a deep love for dark aesthetics, black eyeliner, and the works of Bauhaus, I decided to dedicate my life to preventing the extinction of goth girls. Some call me crazy. I call it purpose.

Key Achievements in Goth Girl Saving:

  • ✅ Convinced 3 people that goth is not just a phase (pending verification)
  • ✅ Created this entire website at risk of my social life
  • ✅ Listened to Cigarettes After Sex on repeat while building this
  • ✅ Survived multiple "but why do you dress like that?" conversations from relatives
  • ✅ Maintained conviction that goth culture will never die (it's immortal, like vampires)
  • ✅ Learned that preservation requires passion, not just aesthetic choices

Personal Note: Yes, this is slightly absurd. Yes, I'm serious about it anyway. Goth culture deserves to be celebrated, documented, and kept alive by people who actually care about it. If that makes me a goth girl saving warrior, then I accept this title with honor. 🖤

Current Status: Still very much on the mission. Not tired yet. Will update once goth girls are officially off the endangered species list.
