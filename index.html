About SGGP

🎭 What is SGGP?

The Save Goth Girl Program is a community-driven initiative dedicated to preserving and celebrating goth culture, aesthetics, and identity. We discuss ideas to keep goth culture thriving, relevant, and accessible to new generations.

💜 Our Mission

To document goth culture, share knowledge, support community members, and explore how goth aesthetics and values continue to evolve while staying true to the subculture's roots.

🌙 Core Values

Inclusivity, creativity, self-expression, individuality, and community. We celebrate all forms of goth: romantic, gothic lolita, cybergoth, Victorian, and everything in between.

Discussion Ideas & Projects

📚 Goth History Archive

Documenting the origins and evolution of goth culture from the 1980s to today. Collecting stories, photos, and cultural moments that shaped the scene.

Culture

👗 Fashion & DIY Guides

Creating tutorials and resources for DIY goth fashion, makeup, and styling. From traditional goth to alternative interpretations.

Fashion

🎵 Music & Playlist Curation

Curating playlists spanning classic goth rock, industrial, darkwave, and contemporary goth-influenced music. Celebrating the soundtrack of goth culture.

💬 Community Stories

Sharing personal stories from goth community members about their journey, identity, and what goth means to them.

🎨 Art & Aesthetics Showcase

Featuring artwork, photography, and visual projects created by and for the goth community. Celebrating goth artistic expression.
